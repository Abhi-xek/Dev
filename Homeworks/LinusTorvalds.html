Biography

Linus Torvaldsis a Finnish-American software engineer who is the creator and principal devloper of the Linux kernel, which is the kernel for a number of operating systems. He also creted the version control system Git. Thorvalds was born on December 28, 1969 in Helsinki, Finland. He studied computer science at the University of Helsinki, and he developed the Linex kernel as a student project in 1991. Since then, the Linux kernel has become one of the most widely used kernel in the world, powering everything from smartphones to supercomputers.
